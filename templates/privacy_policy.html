Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025.01.01

Introduction

Discovery Jam ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we use the data you provide when using our app. By using Discovery Jam, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Data We Collect

When you use Discovery Jam, we access the following data through the Spotify API: Your Spotify account information (e.g., Your name, username, profile picture, Spotify followers, and public playlists.). Your listening history and playlists. We do not collect, store, or process any additional personal data.

How We Use Your Data

The data accessed from Spotify is used solely to: Analyze your listening history. Generate a playlist featuring lesser-known artists you may like. We do not store, share, or use your data for any other purpose.

Data Retention

We do not store your data. Once the playlist is generated, all accessed data is immediately discarded.

Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the Spotify API, which is governed by Spotify's Privacy Policy. Please review their policy for more information.

User Rights

Since we do not store your data, there is no retained information to access, modify, or delete.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. The updated policy will be posted on this page with the effective date.
